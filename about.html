Welcome to Shree Bhawati Secondary School, a haven of learning established in the year 2017 BS. Rooted in a foundation of excellence, we provide education from pre-nursery to 12th class. Our qualified teachers, possessing master's degrees, create a supportive and good environment for students to flourish. We boast modern amenities including computer and science labs, an enriching library, and a commitment to a strong education system. With a focus on holistic development, our approachable teachers offer friendly guidance, nurturing young minds into well-rounded individuals. Join us in our picturesque campus, where a blend of academic prowess and a good education system fosters success.
